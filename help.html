Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use rebook?

You do not need an account to browse for books but in order to contact a seller, purchase a book or post a book for sale you do need to have a profile.

Is there a fee for using rebook?

No! Rebook is absolutely free of charge :)

Do I need to meet a seller in person to purchase a book?

If the seller is offering in-person pickup, it will be indicated on the listing. If shipping is chosen as an option instead, costs are to be covered by the buyer.

How do I know what condition the book is in?

Our books are rated as either new, great, good, or poor condition. We trust that sellers will be honest about the condition of a book and include detailed comments about any imperfections that buyers should know about. We encourage asking for photos of the exterior and interior of the book if you are unsure.
