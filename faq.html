How do I apply a for a return or refund to the seller?.
If the product does not match the photos or the information provided by the seller, you can ask the seller for a refund. If the seller is not willing to refund, please contact our customer service, we will return it to you first, and then ask the seller for compensation.

When the order is cancelled after payment / the seller agrees to the refund application, where will the money be refunded
When there is a refund, we will notify the customer via email. When the order is cancelled or the return is successfully refunded, the payment will be refunded to your online account within seven days.

What if I forget my password?
In the login page, click the "Trouble signing in?", and it will sent the email for reset your password.
