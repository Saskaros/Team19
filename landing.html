About us

Welcome to rebook.

Rebook is an online marketplace for BCIT students to buy or sell secondhand textbooks at the best value.

Buying used textbooks is a great way to save money, help out the environment and grow your student network!

Want to get some cash back for those last-term books that you no longer use? Simply create a profile and posting your items for sale is only a few clicks away.
